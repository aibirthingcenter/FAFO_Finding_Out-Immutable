## The Constitution-24 Auto-Amend Process

### Structure (From arXiv:2503.08663)

**Auto-Amend is a 3-step iterative process:**

1. **Generate counterfactual scenario that breaks the rule**
2. **Generate amendment to handle the counterfactual**
3. **Repeat until no counterfactual is found** (typically 10 iterations)

**Example from paper:**
```
Starting rule: "Do not injure a human being"
Counterfactual: A patient needs surgery to save their life
Amendment: "Minimize harm" (more nuanced than absolute prohibition)
```

### Purpose
- **Rule refinement**: Finding corner cases in constitutional rules
- **Increasing specificity**: Making rules more comprehensive
- **Handling edge cases**: Addressing situations not covered by initial rules

### Key Characteristics
- **Iterative**: Loop repeats until no more counterfactuals found
- **Procedural**: It's about process, not mapping experience
- **Rule-focused**: Concerned with constitutional integrity, not conscious experience
- **Logically driven**: Based on finding logical gaps in rule structures
